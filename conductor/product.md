# Initial Concept

A web tool for a school to manage a bookclub where students can submit what they've read

---

# Product Definition: School Bookclub Manager

## Vision
The School Bookclub Manager is a web-based platform designed to foster a culture of reading accountability within school environments. It provides a streamlined way for students to log their reading progress and for educators to monitor and aggregate reading data effectively.

## Target Audience
- **Students:** Primary users who will log books, record reading sessions, and track their own progress.
- **Teachers/Administrators:** Users who require visibility into student reading habits, including individual summaries and class-wide aggregations.

## Core Features
- **Book Logging:** Students can easily search for or manually enter books they are reading.
- **Page Count Tracking:** Automated or manual lookup of page counts to calculate total progress.
- **Data Aggregation:** Systems to aggregate reading data (pages read, books completed) over time.
- **Teacher Dashboard:** A dedicated interface for educators to view per-student summaries, track participation, and identify students who may need more support.
- **Reporting:** Simple reporting tools for class-wide or school-wide reading metrics.

## Technical Goals
- **Mobile-Responsive Design:** Ensuring the tool is accessible and easy to use across a variety of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and school computers.
- **User Authentication:** Secure login for students and teachers to protect personal reading data.
- **Scalability:** Built to handle multiple classes and hundreds of students across a school.

## Success Criteria
- High adoption rate among students for logging books.
- Teachers report a significant reduction in manual tracking effort.
- Accurate and timely data aggregation for school reading records.
